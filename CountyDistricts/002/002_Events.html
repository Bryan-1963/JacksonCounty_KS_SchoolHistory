1869:

The Holton High School reported that they had over 100 students in attendance (Ref 24)

April 1872:

The Holton Express reported results of school office elections and their plan to enclose the school grounds with a "substantial, neat-looking fence." The officers also placed an advertisement to recruit students for the term beginning April 15th (Ref 17).

Major Anderson was elected School Director

J.L. Peck was elected School Clerk

D.W.C. Locke was elected School Treasurer
