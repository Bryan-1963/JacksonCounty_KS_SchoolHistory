1901:

Reference 41 contains photos of the 1900-1901 school year souvenir book from Banner School. Information contained in the book includes:

  1. Rose Sweaney was the teacher.
  2. B.T. Wible was the school director
  3. F. Breitkreutz was the school clerk
  4. William Sanderson was the school treasurer
  5.
