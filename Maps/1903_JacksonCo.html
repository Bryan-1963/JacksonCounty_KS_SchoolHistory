Figure 1 – Locations of Schools on 1903 Map of Jackson County with Modern Road Names (Ref 12)


Notes for 1903 Jackson County

  1. Banner Township did not yet exist, most of what is now Banner Township was the eastern portion of Grant Township.
  2. The town of Delia did not yet exist.
  3. School district 67 was not identified on this map, but is on the Washington Township map.
  4. School district 49 was not identified on this map, but is on the Garfield Township map.

Figure 9 – Location of School on 1903 Map of Circleville City (Ref 12)


Notes for Circleville City:

  1. The school location is not called out on this map. However, the map of Jefferson Township shows the location of the “seminary” and in 1903 the former seminary building was being used as the District 16 school house. The location of the seminary is designated as the location of the school house in this figure.
